Suspicious chest pain would indicate chest pain that may be associated with ACS or pulmonary embolism:
  • Symptoms similar to previous anginal episodes
  • Substernal, "crushing" chest pain or pressure
  • Associated with nausea/vomiting
  • Associated with shortness of breath
  • Associated tachycardia or heart palpitations
  • Radiation of pain into L arm or shoulder
  • Lower extremity edema
  • Associated hemoptysis
